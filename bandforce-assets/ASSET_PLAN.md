## Image Sourcing Strategy

### FREE Sources (100% Commercial Use)
1. **Unsplash** - https://unsplash.com/s/photos/resistance-bands
2. **Pexels** - https://www.pexels.com/search/resistance%20band/
3. **Pixabay** - https://pixabay.com/search/resistance%20band/
4. **Freepik** (free tier) - https://www.freepik.com/free-photos-vectors/resistance-band

### Important Criteria
- ✅ **NO brand logos** on the bands themselves
- ✅ **No visible brand names** (we'll use BandForce instead)
- ✅ **Solid colors** (Yellow, Red, Green, Black preferred)
- ✅ **Professional quality** (at least 800px wide)
- ✅ **Clear, isolated products** (white/neutral background preferred)
- ✅ **Multiple angles** per product (front, side, stretched)

---

## Download Instructions

### For Each Image:
1. Find on free stock site
2. Download full resolution (1200+ px wide)
3. Rename following format: `{product}-{color/type}-{shot-number}.jpg`
4. Place in appropriate folder
5. Update checkboxes above

### Naming Convention:
- Individual bands: `band-{resistance}-{color}-{number}.jpg`
  - Example: `band-light-yellow-01.jpg`
- Sets: `set-{type}-{number}.jpg`
  - Example: `set-loop-4pack-01.jpg`
- Accessories: `accessory-{name}-{number}.jpg`
  - Example: `accessory-door-anchor-01.jpg`
- Action: `action-{exercise}-{gender}-{number}.jpg`
  - Example: `action-bicep-curl-woman-01.jpg`
